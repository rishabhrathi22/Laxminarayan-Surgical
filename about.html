About Us

Overview

Laxminarayan Surgical Pharma is a friendly and professional organisation engaged in wholesale and Retail of all kinds of surgical disposables, health care products and surgical instruments.

Our products find a wide application in hospitals, dispensaries, homes for various medical and surgical purposes. Our range is appreciated in the market for high quality materials, durability and low maintenance.


We Are Trusted Company

We are engaged in offering surgical disposables, health care products and surgical instruments. Our aim is to serve the customers with high quality range of products. We are emerged as top supplier in Tamilnadu state within short period of time with our good products range, competitive price, good services and a better quality products. We have a very large no of customer portfolio (Wholesaler & Retailer), nursing homes, hospitals, institutes & small clinics.

We are Authorized distributors of MEDINORM, AIRWAYS SURGICAL & ANSUYA SURGICALS for Tamilnadu state. We are also stockiest for Johnson and Johnson, BSN, JMS, Sutures India, 3M, B-Braun, Covidien, BD, tynor, smith and nephew, Dynamic techno medicals, Raman and weil private ltd., accu-chek and surgicom.
